Objective/Goals

The objective of our mini site is to promote mental and physical well-being. It will achieve this by providing resources and activities that encourage visitors to develop new interests, engage in physical activities, and stimulate their intellectual thinking. We aim to create an interactive platform that inspires and motivates our audience.

Key Audience(s)

Students

College and school students looking for fun and educational activities.

Working professionals

Individuals seeking ways to balance work and leisure.

Parents

Parents searching for engaging activities for their children.

Seniors

Senior citizens interested in staying active and mentally sharp.

Provisional Site Structure

Home

Introduction and featured activities.

Activities

List of physical and mental activities with detailed descriptions.

Resources

A collection of articles, videos, and books for intellectual stimulation.

Blog

User-generated content and stories about their experiences.

Contact

A form to get in touch with us.

Functional Specification

  • Interactive activities should include quizzes, workouts, and puzzles.
  • Users can submit their own content to the blog.
  • Activity suggestions should be filterable by difficulty level and duration.
  • The Contact Us form should collect user inquiries.
